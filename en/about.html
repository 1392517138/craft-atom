The origin of the name

From 1998 to 2008, i have a crush on two games StarCraft and WarCraft. One day i became a professional programmer, i spent more and more time on programming not gaming. I miss deeply those StarCraft or WarCraft past days, so i use the 'Craft' to naming this project to commemorate past days. More importantly, i hope this project to inherit the spirit of the two craft games.
